Rocket

In development

N-1 rocket

N-1 rocket is our first rocket powered by a solid rocket motor that uses sugar and potassium nitrate as the propellant. The airframe is 3D printed and equips the minimum required flight electronics. Through the development of the N-1 rocket, we aim to acquire basic rocketry techniques and establish a safety launch sequence.

N-2 rocket

Our next target is to develop the N-2 rocket, which can bring our payload up to 300m altitudes. The casing and the nozzle of the solid rocket motor are made with aluminum. It also demonstrates the performance of our flight controller to stabilize the rocket using servos and fins. The payload camera will capture the movie during flight. The payload will be recovered by parachute and telemetry system using GPS.
